The website should feel less like a traditional portfolio and more like an exploration of a curious builder's mind. The design should be minimal, premium, and slightly experimental, with smooth animations, microinteractions, and a strong focus on storytelling.

GENERAL THEME

The core narrative of the website is:

"I build things because I'm curious."

The site should showcase a unique combination of technology, automation, journalism, public speaking, geopolitics, and community leadership. The goal is for visitors to understand that I am not just a developer, but someone who explores ideas and turns them into products.

Use a clean modern aesthetic with generous whitespace, smooth transitions, subtle motion effects, and elegant typography. Avoid overly corporate designs.
